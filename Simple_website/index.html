However there was a big learning curve , it was up to myself to make sure that I keep track of all my classes.Unlike highschool were teacher constatnly remind you of what is due and what you need to do, University you must take it on yourself to make sure that you undestand what is going on in the class, otherwise you risk falling behind.Making new friends was also a great experience at the University,many different people from all around the world coming together to learn and improve themselves creates a pleasing environment

But here professors seem to really care about feedback and always try helping students when they can.Overall I've been satisfied with the teaching experience at the University of windsor.
